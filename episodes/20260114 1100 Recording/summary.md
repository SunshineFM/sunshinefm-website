## Summary

This SunshineFM episode explores the widening gap between AI readiness among the elite and the slow integration of AI education in mainstream schools. The host discusses how billionaires are preparing for an uncertain AI-driven future by investing in bunkers and private academies. They emphasize that while AI tools are becoming more accessible, the key to thriving in an AI-dominated world lies in fostering a culture of experimentation and building an 'I can build' mindset from a young age. The episode calls for a shift in educational priorities to equip students with the skills and confidence to navigate the rapidly changing technological landscape.

### Key Insights

- Billionaires are preparing for potential societal instability related to AI by building bunkers and securing resources.
- The education system is lagging in AI integration, leading wealthy individuals to create private academies for their children.
- Open-source AI democratizes access to tools, but mindset and a culture that embraces experimentation are crucial for success.
- The 'I can build' mindset is more important than just access to AI tools.
